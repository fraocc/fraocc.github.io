Francesca Occhiuto

About Me

I am a PhD student in Veterinary Science at the University of Nottingham, UK. My research focuses on individual variation in the behaviour of cattle using precision livestock technologies. I use SQL for data management and R for data analysis. I have published several papers in scientific journals.

Research

My research interests include:

  • Animal Behaviour
  • Animal Personality
  • Precision Livestock Technology
  • Data Analysis with R
  • Database Management with SQL

Publications

  • Occhiuto, F., Vázquez-Diosdado, J.A., King, A.J. and Kaler J., 2023. Evidence of personality-dependent plasticity in dairy calf movement behaviours derived from automated data collection. Sci Rep 13, 18243. https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-023-44957-z
